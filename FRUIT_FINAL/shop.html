Found by two summer lovin' owners, Violet and Viola, Fruitastic is born in the sunny island of Singapore, where it is summer whole year round.
To beat the scortching summer heat without endulging in sinful ice cream, we provide an alternative solution to you.
Hence, fruitastic pop is born. Our products are made of non-preservatives, coloring, artificial flavour and fruit fresh delivered daily to provide you the freshest ice cream.


Homemade with love

All fruit popsicles are made first thing in the morning with freshly imported ingredients from all over the world. We can guarantee this will be the freshest popsicle you will ever find in the market!

Delivery fee capped

We understand that delivery can be expensive, hence all deliveries island-wide that are reachable to us will be capped at $5!

We value feedback

In order to bring the best quality of popsicles to you, we read all of your feedback and we are constantly improving ourselves to keep you satisfied!

Keeping up with the trend

We are constantly upgrading ourselves to bring you more variety of popsicles as well as different fruits all around the world. Keep a look out for our newest additions!
