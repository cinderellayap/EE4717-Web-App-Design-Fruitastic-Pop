Frequency Asked Questions

Are your ingredients all natural?
Yes, all our ingredients are natural with no preservatives, additional coloring or artificial flavour added.
Do you deliver to all parts of Singapore?
We do deliver to all parts of Singapore except Pulau Ubin.
When is the earliest I can get my popsicles?
If you order before 3pm the day before, you can get your popsicle delivered the next working day. If orders are sent after 3pm, it will be delivered two working days later.
Do I get a free delivery if I order in bulk?
For orders above $80, delivery will be on us!
